# La 3ème Dimension at Nuit des Étoiles 2026, at the Cité de l'espace

On **Friday, August 7, 2026**, the Cité de l'espace in Toulouse opens its doors for an evening event for **Nuit des Étoiles** (Night of the Stars), organized by the French Astronomy Association around the theme *"From nights of stars to the night of the Star."* Sky watching, workshops, shows, and above all the **Perseids** — the most spectacular meteor shower of the year.

**La 3ème Dimension** will be there to run a workshop.

## Workshop: Build your own telescope

How does a simple refractor let you observe the stars? On the **Belvédère d'Ariane**, kids and adults assemble a "telescope" step by step and discover the role each lens plays in forming the image.

Open to everyone, recommended from age 6. Ongoing, drop-in access all evening — no booking required.

## The rest of the program

The evening is packed, with plenty of free, drop-in activities in the gardens and inside the Astralia building:

- **Sky observation with telescopes** (gardens), with Planète Sciences Occitanie
- **The Astronomer's Dome**: observing the Sun magnified up to 20 times
- **"Sun Earth, Moon" activity** and **"Tales and legends"**
- **"Make your own sky map" workshop**
- **"Invitation au vertige" show** (aerial circus, Terrain Martien) at 9:15pm, 10:15pm, and 11:15pm
- **Astronomy quiz**, lightsaber demonstration, Méliès' short film *A Trip to the Moon*

Some sessions (IMAX, Planetarium, Stellarium) require separate online registration, subject to availability.

## Practical information

- **Date:** Friday, August 7, 2026
- **Hours:** 8:30pm – 12:30am (last admission at 11:45pm)
- **Price:** free, but **online registration is mandatory for every participant**, regardless of age
- **Registration opens:** Monday, July 20 at 10am
- **Location:** Cité de l'espace, avenue Jean Gonord, 31500 Toulouse, France

The full program and registration are available on the event's official page: [cite-espace.com](https://www.cite-espace.com/a-la-une/la-nuit-des-etoiles-2026/).

If you're in Toulouse that evening, come build your own refractor with us on the Belvédère d'Ariane!

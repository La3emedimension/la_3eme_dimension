The DR-01 (<a href="https://support.apple.com/en-us/105047">MagSafe*</a>) keeps the design ideas of the Braun DN 40 alarm clock by Dieter Rams adapted for iPhone with MagSafe charger. Scott Yu-Jan's project and video gave us the idea to optimize the concept.

The DR-01 seems much more optimized for 3D printing. It adapts to different iPhone sizes with MagSafe and all are compatible. The object is in 2 parts: the phone stand and a screw to secure the <a href="https://support.apple.com/en-us/105047">MagSafe*</a> cable.

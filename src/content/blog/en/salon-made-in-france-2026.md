# La 3ème Dimension at the Made in France Show 2026

From **November 12 to 15, 2026**, **La 3ème Dimension** will be at **MIF Expo**, the Made in France show, in Paris — Porte de Versailles.

## The Craft Village

The workshop will be exhibiting within the **Village de l'Artisanat** (Craft Village), the pavilion dedicated entirely to craftsmanship and run by the network of French Chambers of Trade and Crafts (CMA): over 200 artisans from every region of France (mainland and overseas), across 2,000 m² dedicated to local know-how.

La 3ème Dimension represents Occitan craftsmanship there, in the Leisure category, on behalf of the Tarn-et-Garonne department: designing and manufacturing compact astronomical instruments, built to make observing the sky simple and accessible to everyone.

## Practical information

- **Dates:** Thursday, November 12 to Sunday, November 15, 2026
- **Hours:** Thursday 12th, Friday 13th and Saturday 14th of November, 10am to 7pm — Sunday, November 15th, 10am to 6pm
- **Location:** Paris, Porte de Versailles — Hall 7.2
- **Price:** free with pre-registration, €10 on site (refunded on your first purchase)

⚠️ Don't buy your tickets from a street vendor: they won't be accepted at the entrance to the show.

All information and the exhibitor listing are available on the [Village de l'Artisanat website](https://village.artisanat.fr/artisan/tarn-et-garonne-la-3-eme-dimension/).

If you're in Paris that November weekend, come discover the Smallest, the Lumi, and our other instruments at the booth!

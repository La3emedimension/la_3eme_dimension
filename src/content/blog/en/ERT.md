E.R.T is an educational project to discover optics and some sky objects, moon, bright galaxies, star clusters...

---
It consists of several modules:

- Lens barrel.
- Stray light baffle.
- Optical tube.
- 1.25 inch focuser.
- Diagonal with binocular prism.
- Eyepiece.

All these elements have only one mounting direction and assembly is easy as shown in the video and drawing below:
